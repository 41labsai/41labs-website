What is AI quote automation?

AI quote automation is software that reads an enquiry, works out the price from your own rules, and produces a ready-to-send quote in minutes instead of hours. The enquiry can arrive as an email, a PDF, a WhatsApp message, a spec sheet, or a bill of quantities. The AI reads it, matches the items to your catalogue and pricing, applies your discounts and margins, and drafts the quote in your own format.

The important word is custom. A generic quoting tool asks you to bend your business to fit its fields. We do the opposite. We train the system on your products, your pricing logic, and the messy real enquiries your customers actually send. Your team stays in control. The AI does the reading, the lookups, and the typing. A person checks the final quote and sends it. That is how you get speed without losing the judgement that wins the job.

Which Singapore businesses need it most?

The firms that quote by hand and quote often see the biggest gain.

Renovation & construction

You price from drawings, BOQs, and site measurements, and every quote takes hours. AI reads the BOQ, matches line items to your rate card, and drafts the quote so you reply the same day instead of three days later.

Freight & logistics

You quote shipments across carriers, lanes, and surcharges that change weekly. AI reads the shipment details, pulls the right rates, and builds the quote in minutes, so no enquiry sits in an inbox overnight.

Suppliers & manufacturers who quote by hand

You price from parts lists, catalogues, and customer-specific pricing. AI reads the request, applies the right tier and margin, and formats a clean quote, without the copy-paste errors that cost you money later.

How it works in 4 to 8 weeks

Most quote automation systems go from first call to live in 4 to 8 weeks, depending on how complex your pricing is and how many systems the quote pulls from. Here is the path.

  • Week 1, we learn how you quote. We sit with your team, collect real enquiries, and map your pricing rules, discounts, and formats.
  • Weeks 2 to 3, you get a free working demo. We build a demo on your real data so you can see a real quote drafted from a real enquiry. You decide to continue only after you have seen it work.
  • Weeks 3 to 6, we build the full system. We connect it to your price lists, catalogues, and systems, and we handle the awkward edge cases that break generic tools.
  • Weeks 6 to 8, we go live with your team. Your staff test it on live enquiries, we fine-tune, and we hand over a system your team trusts.

Every step is founder-led. You work directly with the people building the system, not a junior who was briefed second-hand.
