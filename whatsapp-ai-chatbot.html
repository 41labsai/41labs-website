WhatsApp AI Chatbot — FAQ

What is a WhatsApp AI chatbot?

A WhatsApp AI chatbot is an AI agent that handles customer conversations on WhatsApp automatically — answering questions, sending quotes, qualifying leads, and booking sales 24/7. A custom one, like 41 Labs builds, is trained on your products and pricing so it replies accurately and in your brand voice, instead of using rigid scripted menus.

How is it different from a normal WhatsApp Business auto-reply?

WhatsApp Business auto-replies are fixed canned messages. A custom AI agent holds a real conversation, understands your catalogue and pricing, generates quotes, qualifies the buyer, and escalates hot leads to your team — it sells, it doesn't just acknowledge.

Can it generate quotes and book appointments on WhatsApp?

Yes. The agent can generate accurate quotes from your pricing rules and book appointments or orders directly in the chat, then send reminders and follow-ups automatically.

Is this good for Singapore and Southeast Asia?

Especially. WhatsApp is the dominant channel for customer conversations across Singapore and SEA, so an agent that replies there instantly in your brand voice captures leads a web form would lose.
