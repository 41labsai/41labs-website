Brand Book

Guidelines for maintaining a consistent brand identity across all 41 Labs touchpoints.

Version 1.0 — February 2026

01 — Logo

Our Mark

The 41 Labs logo combines a stylized "A1" monogram with the "Labs" wordmark. Use the full logo wherever possible.

Primary Logo

Use on light backgrounds. Default for all applications.

On Dark Backgrounds

Invert to white when placing on dark surfaces.

Icon Mark

Use for favicons, app icons, and small formats.

Maintain clear space

Always keep a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "1" character.

Use provided files

Only use official logo files. Never recreate or approximate the logo.

Don't distort or rotate

Never stretch, compress, rotate, or alter the proportions of the logo.

Don't add effects

No drop shadows, gradients, outlines, or visual effects on the logo itself.
