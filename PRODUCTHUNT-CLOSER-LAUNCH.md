## 9. Do and Don't

**Do**
- Reply to every comment, fast and for real.
- Text the demo yourself and invite everyone to text it too. The reply is the demo.
- Be honest about what it does and does not do.
- Thank people by name.
- Share your launch openly on your own channels.
- Engage genuinely with other launches that day.

**Don't**
- Do not ask directly for upvotes. It is against Product Hunt rules. Frame everything around trying the product.
- Do not buy votes or use fake accounts or vote rings.
- Do not offer anything in exchange for a vote.
- Do not spam DMs. Send real messages to real people, in waves.
- Do not argue with critics. Answer honestly and move on.
- Do not post and disappear. Presence in the comments is the whole game.
