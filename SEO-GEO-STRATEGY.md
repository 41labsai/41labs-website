## Part 3: GEO Strategy (Generative Engine Optimization)

### What Makes Content "Citation-Worthy" for AI Engines

AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) cite content that has:

1. **Statistics & Data** - Specific numbers, percentages, ROI figures
2. **Expert Quotes** - Attributed statements from named experts
3. **Structured Answers** - Clear H2/H3 hierarchy, bullet points, tables
4. **Authoritative Tone** - Definitive statements, not wishy-washy
5. **Source Citations** - Links to studies, reports, credible sources
6. **Schema Markup** - FAQPage, HowTo, Article structured data

### Current 41 Labs GEO Score: 6/10

**Strengths:**
- Good structured content (tables, bullets)
- Clear expertise signals (founder-led, case studies)
- Schema on some pages (HowTo on process.html)

**Gaps to Fix:**
- Need more statistics/data points in blog posts
- Add FAQPage schema to service pages
- Include more "expert quote" style callouts
- Add more external citations to credible sources
