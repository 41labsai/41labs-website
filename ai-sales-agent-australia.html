AI Sales Agent Australia — FAQ

What is an AI sales agent?

An AI sales agent is software that handles sales conversations automatically — answering product and pricing questions, generating quotes, and qualifying leads. A custom agent like 41 Labs builds is trained on your real catalogue and pricing, so it gives accurate answers instead of generic chatbot responses, and runs on WhatsApp and web chat 24/7.

What is the best AI sales agent for an Australian SME?

Global platforms like Salesforce or Drift suit large sales teams with standard processes and in-house people to run them. For an Australian SME with a specific catalogue and pricing, a custom AI sales agent trained on your own products — like the ones 41 Labs builds — usually converts better, because it answers accurately instead of guessing, and it is fully managed rather than another tool your team has to operate.

How is an AI sales agent different from a chatbot?

A chatbot follows scripted flows or gives generic answers. A custom AI sales agent understands your products, prices, and policies, holds a natural conversation, generates real quotes, and qualifies leads before handing them to your team. The difference is accuracy and outcome — sales, not just chat.

Does the AI sales agent answer phone calls?

No. 41 Labs builds text-based AI sales agents that work on WhatsApp and web chat. It does not answer phone calls or act as a voice receptionist. If your enquiries arrive mainly by phone, we are not the right fit and we will tell you so on the first call.

Which channels does the AI sales agent work on in Australia?

WhatsApp and web chat. Australian businesses that sell internationally, serve migrant communities, or already run enquiries through WhatsApp get the most value. If your customers reach you through your website chat, the agent handles those enquiries the same way — answering, quoting, and qualifying automatically.

A custom AI sales agent from 41 Labs typically falls in the S$7,000 to S$25,000 range (Singapore dollars) depending on scope and integrations, and managed plans start from S$690 per month. You get a free working prototype built on your own products and pricing before you commit any budget.
