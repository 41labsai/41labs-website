## The 3 objections (state, then knock down)
1. **"My prices are secret and per-customer."** Real (Buildmate/Hafary hide prices behind enquiry flow). Answer: AI never guesses; gives the account's agreed price or captures the lead and pings the boss. Never shows walk-in price to a trade account.
2. **"Stock changes hourly — it'll promise stock I don't have."** Real (ready-stock vs indent). Answer: it says "let me confirm and come back," or reads the live sheet if connected. Never over-promises.
3. **"Credit terms differ per account."** Real (COD vs 30/60/90). Answer: knows each account's terms; routes anything new to a human. New buyer asking credit = flagged, not auto-approved.

## Where the money leaks (the heart of the pitch)
- Average business takes **42 hours** to reply to a new lead; only **23%** reply within 5 min. [SOURCED — HBR, 2,241 companies]
- Reply in 5 min = **21x** more likely to win vs 30 min. [SOURCED — MIT/InsideSales]
- Whoever replies first often wins (~35–50%). [SOURCED — HBR; higher figures secondary]
- Loss modes: after-hours/Sunday, staff serving a walk-in, slow to quote, forgot to follow up, contractor blasts 3 shops at once.
