## The two conclusions that shape everything
1. **Local SG terms are winnable on quality, not quantity.** chatbot.com.sg ranks #1 with 1 link → for "whatsapp chatbot singapore" a stronger page + a few real citations wins. Do NOT try to match moobicast's 102 junk links.
2. **The moobicast link profile is a warning, not a template.** Their links are directory spam and PBNs. Replicating that risks a penalty and wastes effort. Per our own playbook (Quaid method + CLAUDE.md): **build fewer, real, editorially-earned links + co-citation.** ~20-30 genuine local business citations will out-rank 100 junk links.

## Target: get from 7 → ~25-30 QUALITY referring domains in 90 days
Not 102. Real ones. Prioritized by effort:effort ratio.

### Tier 1 — Free, high-trust, do first (weeks 1-2)
Real Singapore business directories + platforms Google trusts. These are citations, not PBNs.
- **Google Business Profile** (if not fully optimized) — the single most important local citation.
- **Singapore business directories:** sgpbusiness.com, Yellow Pages SG, Streetdirectory, SGX-adjacent listings, Kompass SG.
- **Startup/tech ecosystem:** e27.co (company profile + potential feature), Tech in Asia company profile, Vulcan Post (SG startup coverage), SGInnovate ecosystem.
- **Software review platforms** (where WhatsApp-AI buyers actually look): G2, Capterra, GetApp, Product Hunt (41 Closer launch — kit already exists at `PRODUCTHUNT-CLOSER-LAUNCH.md`), SourceForge.
- **AI/SaaS directories:** There's an AI Agency, AI tool directories, futuretools-style lists (co-citation value — our GEO memory notes these).

### Tier 2 — Earned editorial (weeks 3-8, the real authority)
These move the needle most because they're editorial, not directory.
- **Guest posts / expert quotes** in SG business + tech media: Vulcan Post, e27, The Straits Times tech/SME section, SME-focused outlets. Angle: "Why Singapore SMEs are moving sales to WhatsApp" — data-led, not promotional.
- **HARO / Qwoted / SourceBottle** — respond to journalist queries on AI/automation/WhatsApp business; earns links from real publications.
- **Partnerships:** co-marketing with complementary (non-competing) SG SaaS — accounting tools, CRMs, POS systems that our clients use. Cross-links + co-citation.
- **Podcasts / interviews:** founder (Alexander) on SG business/startup podcasts. Show notes = a real link + brand mention (co-citation gold for GEO).

### Tier 3 — Content that attracts links (ongoing)
- The **comparison pages already rank pos 3-6** and are naturally linkable. Pitch them to "best WhatsApp tools" listicles (outreach list exists: `geo/listicle-outreach-list.md`).
