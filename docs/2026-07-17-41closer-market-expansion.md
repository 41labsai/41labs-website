**Key signal:** local AU players (aidial.com.au, sophiie.ai, digitalon.ai, withallo) already rank — proving AU SMEs buy AI phone/reception agents and the category isn't locked by giants. First-mover room exists.

### ⚠️ The catch that decides HOW we enter AU
**Australia is WhatsApp-light.** "whatsapp business api" = 320 AU vs 720 SG / 1,300 MY. Aussies run business on **phone, SMS, email** — not WhatsApp. So:
- 41 Closer's **WhatsApp-first framing does NOT travel to AU.**
- AU demand is for **"AI receptionist / AI answering service / AI voice agent"** — i.e. the **phone/voice** version of the product.
- **Entering AU means leading with the voice/phone-agent angle, not WhatsApp.** If 41 Closer can answer calls (or we position the WhatsApp+web-chat version as an "AI receptionist for enquiries"), AU is open. If it's strictly WhatsApp, AU is a weaker fit and MY/PH come first.

## The market priority stack (given SG-first, regional-capable)
Ranked by fit × winnable-volume × channel-match:

1. **Singapore** (home / proof) — small volume, but trust + local terms + the comparison moat. Win the ~5 winnable terms + GEO. *Already the plan.*
2. **Malaysia** — ⭐ **best pure-WhatsApp expansion.** "whatsapp business api" 1,300 (bigger than SG!), same product, same framing, just localize. Lowest-effort, highest-fit expansion. Do this FIRST.
3. **Philippines** — huge on "lead generation" (2,900) + "conversational ai" (22,200) + WhatsApp-friendly. Big volume, but qualify: lower deal sizes, BPO-heavy market. Good for volume/awareness, watch monetization.
4. **Australia** — biggest per-term $ value (CPCs $16-236) + winnable KD, BUT requires the **voice/phone-agent framing**, not WhatsApp. Enter only if we can position 41 Closer as an "AI receptionist/answering service." High-value if we can.
