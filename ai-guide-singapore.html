Frequently Asked Questions

How do Singapore businesses start with AI?

Identify your single highest-pain process — usually quoting, document processing, customer replies, or scheduling — and quantify the hours it costs weekly. Get a working prototype on your real data before committing budget. SMEs can offset up to 50% of cost through the EDG grant.

How much does AI cost for a Singapore SME?

Off-the-shelf tools cost S$50–S$500/month; a custom system for an SME costs S$7,000–S$25,000; enterprise projects start at S$50,000+. The EDG grant covers up to 50% (70% for eligible SMEs).

Is there government funding for AI in Singapore?

Yes — the Enterprise Development Grant (EDG) covers up to 50% of qualifying AI costs (up to 70% for eligible SMEs), administered by Enterprise Singapore. Your business must be registered and operating in Singapore with at least 30% local shareholding.
