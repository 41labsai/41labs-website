Your questions, answered

Everything a good partner asks first.

How and when do I get paid?

We pay by bank transfer once your client has paid us. Your upfront share lands after their first invoice clears. Your recurring share is paid monthly while they stay, for twelve months. You get a simple statement each month showing what you earned.

How do you track my referrals?

You introduce the client to us by name, or send them with your partner link. We tag the deal to you the moment it comes in. There is no race and no cookie window to worry about. Once a client is yours, they are yours.

What if my client cancels?

You keep everything you have already earned. The recurring share simply stops when they stop paying. There is never a clawback on commission we have already sent you.

Is there a cost or a minimum to join?

No cost, and no minimum number of referrals. Join, refer when you have a good fit, and earn. There are no fees and no quotas.

Will you go around me and contact my client directly?

Never. We close and deliver under 41 Labs, but the relationship stays yours, and we never sell your client anything you did not bring us. We protect the partner who brought the deal.

Which products can I earn on?

Our recurring AI products. 41 Closer, the WhatsApp AI sales agent. 41 Leads, lead generation. 41 GEO, getting your client recommended by ChatGPT and Perplexity. Anything with a monthly fee pays you a recurring share.

Do I need to know anything about AI to refer?

No. You bring the relationship and the introduction. We bring the demo, the pitch, the build, and the support. If you can spot a client who is losing sales or asking about AI, you can earn.
