What does it cost?

From $690 a month, plus a one-time build fee from $4,800. Pay monthly by card or bank transfer and cancel any time. Pay for the year and get two months free.

What is the build fee for, and why is Pro's higher?

The build fee is a one-time cost to set it up. We train it on your products and prices, give it your way of talking, connect WhatsApp and payment, and switch it on. Pro is higher because we also clean and load your full catalogue for you. The monthly fee is for running it and keeping it sharp.

What do I need to give you?

A list of your products and prices, and a bit about how you sell. That is it. We do everything else.

What happens if I get more chats than my plan?

Nothing bad. We just add more, and we tell you before you hit the limit. No shock bills, ever.

Does it use my own WhatsApp number?

Yes. It runs on your business WhatsApp. Your customers see your brand, not ours.

What languages does it speak?

English and the way Singapore really chats, including a mix of languages. We tune it to sound like your business.

How fast is it live?

48 hours. You send your product list, we build it, connect WhatsApp, and switch it on.

Can I cancel, and is my data safe?

Yes to both. Plans are month to month with no lock-in. You own the agent and everything it learns, and we never share or sell your data.

What if it doesn't work?

You watch it sell your own products first, for free, before you pay. And in your first month, if it does not bring in sales you would have missed, that month is free.
