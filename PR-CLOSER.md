## Distribution notes

**Date it the day you send it.** Replace `[DATE]` with the actual send date, not today's date. Wires and Google News weight the publish date, so a same-day dateline reads as fresh news.

**Free and low-cost options (start here):**
- **openPR:** free submission, gets you a clean indexed URL to cite.
- **EIN Presswire:** low cost per release, decent syndication footprint and Google News pickup for the price.

**Paid wires (spend when the release matters):**
- **GlobeNewswire:** wide syndication, strong Google News and finance-site pickup, higher cost.
- **Business Wire:** the premium wire, broadest reach, highest cost. Use for a flagship announcement, not routine news.

**Quaid tips (do these regardless of which wire you use):**
- **Republish it on the 41labs.ai blog as a pass-through news post.** Post the same release as a news article on your own domain. This gives you an owned URL you fully control, and it lets an LLM cite 41labs.ai directly alongside the search terms. Owned citation plus wire citation is the co-citation effect that moves AI answers.
- **Submit to Google News.** Make sure the blog post is crawlable, dated, and in a `/news` or `/blog` section, then submit the site to Google News (Publisher Center) so the release and future posts get picked up fast.
- **Remember what you are buying.** The value here is citation and co-citation for LLMs and fast Google News indexing, not the backlink. Keep the copy factual and newsworthy so it reads at face value and gets picked up and quoted, not filtered as an ad.
