From 3 Hours to 5 Minutes: Quote Automation

Professional Services Firm

Key Results

Quote time: 3 hours → 5 minutes
Error rate down 90%
Sales capacity up 35%

The Challenge

Sales team spending 40% of time on manual quote generation

The Solution

Custom AI system connected to CRM, ERP, and product database

The Challenge

A professional services firm in Singapore was struggling with their quoting process. Their sales team spent nearly 40% of their time pulling together quotes — gathering product specs, checking pricing rules, accounting for customer history, and ensuring compliance.

The information existed across multiple systems — their CRM, ERP, product database, and pricing spreadsheets. But piecing it together was manual, tedious, and error-prone.

The Solution

We built a custom AI system that:

  • Connects to all their data sources in real-time
  • Understands their specific products and pricing logic
  • Generates accurate quotes based on customer requirements
  • Flags anything unusual for human review

The key wasn't replacing human judgment — it was eliminating the manual data gathering and calculation that consumed most of the time.

The Results

Within 4 weeks of deployment:

  • Quote generation time: Reduced from 3 hours to 5 minutes
  • Error rate: Down 90%
  • Sales team capacity: Up 35% — now focused on selling, not admin

Key Takeaway

AI isn't about replacing people. It's about eliminating the tedious work so people can focus on what actually requires human intelligence: building relationships, solving complex problems, closing deals.

"What used to take our team 3 hours now takes 5 minutes. The accuracy is remarkable."
— James Tan, COO
