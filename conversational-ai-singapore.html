Conversational AI — FAQ

What is conversational AI?

Conversational AI is technology that lets a computer understand and respond to natural human language, so a business can hold real two-way conversations with customers automatically. It powers AI agents that answer questions, qualify leads, generate quotes, and book sales in natural language, 24/7 — unlike a scripted chatbot that only follows fixed button menus.

What is the difference between conversational AI and a chatbot?

A traditional chatbot follows rigid scripts and keyword rules, so it breaks the moment a customer phrases something unexpectedly. Conversational AI understands intent and context, so it holds a natural conversation, handles follow-up questions, and actually helps the customer — it converses instead of deflecting.

How do Singapore businesses use conversational AI?

The highest-value use in Singapore is on WhatsApp, where customers already message. A conversational AI agent answers product and pricing questions instantly, qualifies buyers, books appointments, and closes sales around the clock. 41 Labs deploys this as a fully managed agent called 41 Closer.

Is conversational AI the same as ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general conversational AI you chat with directly. A business conversational AI agent is different: it is trained on your specific products, pricing, and policies, connected to your systems, and deployed on your channels so it answers accurately as your business — not as a generic assistant.

How much does conversational AI cost in Singapore?
