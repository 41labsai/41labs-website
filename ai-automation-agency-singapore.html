How much does an AI automation agency cost in Singapore?

Custom AI automation typically costs S$7,000–S$25,000 per project. Off-the-shelf tools run S$50–S$500/month; large consultancies charge S$50,000+. The EDG covers up to 50% of qualifying costs (up to 70% for eligible SMEs), reducing a S$15,000 project to as little as S$7,500 out of pocket.

What can an AI automation agency automate?

The highest-ROI automations are quote and proposal generation, document and invoice processing, customer inquiry handling and lead qualification, scheduling and dispatch, financial reconciliation, and inventory forecasting. Any repetitive task involving text, data, or decisions is a candidate.

What's the difference between an AI automation agency and a tool?

A tool is one-size-fits-all and you configure it within its limits. An agency like 41 Labs builds a system around your specific workflows, data, and pricing logic — handling the messy real-world work that off-the-shelf tools break on.

How long does it take to build an AI automation system?

Expect a working prototype on your real data within 2 weeks and full production deployment in 3–8 weeks, depending on complexity. You see value from the prototype before committing budget.
