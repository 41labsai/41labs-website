Who is Analytico?

Analytico AI is a Singapore firm at 30 Cecil Street that builds custom AI agents and autonomous agentic workflows. Its founder and lead developer, Kishan, has 18 years of experience in AI and analytics. The company publishes over a dozen case studies and lists clients including Shangri-La, NTUC, Singapore General Hospital, and IMDA.

Its website lists five service lines: AI reporting and decision intelligence, AI content and growth automation, document and knowledge management, voice and conversation AI agents, and agentic workflow automation. It also sells a product family under names like Analytico Chat, Analytico Voice, and Analytico Dashboards.

Two things stand out in its positioning. First, depth of customisation: the site promises a degree of customisation "most vendors won't offer" and says the firm deliberately works with a small number of clients each year to stay responsive. Second, local language capability: its case studies include a dialect voice agent for a telco, a multilingual voice chat for seniors, Chinese essay grading, and Malay language learning tools. That is a genuinely strong portfolio for Singapore's multilingual reality.

Analytico also runs hands-on workshops, including one where attendees build three working AI agents in an afternoon with no coding experience needed. Pricing and delivery timelines are not published on its site, and it does not mention EDG or other grants, so ask about all three on your first call. It does offer free demos and consultations.

Who is GetAIReady?

Its Singapore page describes the local operation as one of three global hubs, alongside Sydney and London. Its delivery approach has four stages: educate, assess, plan, deliver. Questions to ask any AI vendor before signing

Whichever firm you shortlist, ask these on the first call. Good vendors answer all of them without flinching:

  • Can you show me a system you built that is still running today? Live systems beat case-study PDFs. Ask how many projects shipped and how many are still in production.
  • Who exactly builds my system, and will I speak to them? At many firms the senior person sells and juniors build. Get names.
  • Can I see a result on my own data before I commit? A demo on sample data proves the tech works. A demo on your data proves it works for you.
  • What is the total cost, including monthly running costs after launch? AI systems have ongoing usage costs. A quote that hides them is not a quote.
  • How long until it is live in my business, not in a pilot? Weeks is a good answer for a focused project. Make sure "delivered" means "running."
  • Do you help with the EDG grant application? The paperwork is real work. A vendor who has done it before saves you weeks.
  • How will you handle my customer data under PDPA? Anyone building AI on Singapore business data should have a clear answer ready.
  • What happens after launch? Ask who fixes things when they break and what a change request costs.

Credit where due: all three firms in this comparison clear more of these checks than most of the market. Analytico offers free demos and consultations. GetAIReady publishes prices and puts senior people on every engagement. 41 Labs shows you a working demo on your data before you pay.
