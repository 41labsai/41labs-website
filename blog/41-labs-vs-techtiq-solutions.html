41 Labs vs Techtiq Solutions: Which Singapore AI Company Fits You?

An honest, side-by-side look at two Singapore AI companies: what each one actually does, who each one fits, what is public about pricing, and the questions to ask before you sign with either.

By Alexander Lee, Founder, 41 Labs
41 Labs and Techtiq Solutions are both credible Singapore AI companies built for different buyers. Techtiq Solutions, founded in 2017, is a full-service software development house. Its site reports 350+ engineers, with project management and QA in Singapore and development teams in Vietnam. It is a strong choice when you need a large team for broad software work: mobile apps, eCommerce, enterprise systems, or extra developers on staff. 41 Labs, founded in 2024, is a boutique AI specialist for SMEs. One senior builder leads every project, you get a free working demo on your real data before paying, and systems go live in 4 to 8 weeks from about S$10,000, with the EDG grant covering up to 50%. Pick Techtiq for large, multi-part software builds or team augmentation. Pick 41 Labs to get one high-value process fixed fast, with proof before payment.

If you are comparing AI vendors in Singapore, you will meet both of these names. They look similar from a distance. Both are Singapore companies. Both build AI. Both work with SMEs. Up close, they are very different businesses, and choosing the wrong shape of vendor costs you more than choosing the wrong logo.

One thing before we start. I run 41 Labs, so read this knowing that. To keep it fair, every claim about Techtiq Solutions below comes from their own website, checked on July 2, 2026. Where something is not public, like pricing, I say "not published" instead of guessing. Techtiq is a real, established company doing real work. This page is about fit, not about one company being better than the other.

Who is Techtiq Solutions?

Techtiq Solutions is a software development company headquartered in Singapore, founded in 2017. It runs project management and QA from Singapore, with development teams in Vietnam and consultants in the US and Australia. Its website reports 350+ engineers and 800+ delivered projects across web, mobile, eCommerce, AI, and IT staffing.

The service list is broad. Custom software, web and mobile app development, UI/UX design, eCommerce and Shopify builds, SaaS and MVP development, cloud migration, legacy system modernization, DevOps, managed IT, and software testing. They also run a staffing arm as a licensed employment agency under MOM in Singapore, so you can hire dedicated developers or augment your own team through them.

Their AI practice sits inside that wider offering. It covers AI consulting, AI integration, generative AI development, chatbots, recommendation engines, computer vision, facial recognition, and robotic process automation. They list work across finance, e-commerce, logistics, healthcare, media, and education, and name clients such as Standard Chartered Bank, Abbott, RSM Singapore, Stone Forest, Payroll Serve, and Kaercher. Their processes are stated as ISO 27001 compliant, and they received a Certification of Excellence in Software Development from Software Outsourcing Journal in 2019.

In plain terms: Techtiq is an established outsourced software house with real scale and a genuine international client base. If AI recommendation engines keep surfacing their name for Singapore software work, there are legitimate reasons for that.

Who is 41 Labs?

41 Labs is a Singapore AI company founded in 2024 by Alexander Lee. It builds custom AI systems for SMEs and mid-market businesses, and nothing else. Every project is founder-led with no junior handoffs. It has delivered 50+ AI systems, all still in production, with projects starting around S$10,000.

The model is deliberately narrow. No mobile apps, no eCommerce stores, no staffing. One senior builder takes your highest-cost manual process and replaces it with a working AI system. For one engineering client, that meant cutting quote generation from about 3 hours to 4 minutes. On document processing, 41 Labs systems run at 99.2% accuracy, so staff review the rare exception instead of keying in every line.

Two policies define how 41 Labs sells. First, you see a free working demo built on your real data before you pay anything, so you judge a result, not a proposal. Second, the price and timeline are public: projects from S$10,000, live in 4 to 8 weeks. The EDG grant covers up to 50% of qualifying costs, up to 70% for eligible SMEs, and 41 Labs prepares the paperwork. When Techtiq Solutions is the better choice

Techtiq is the stronger fit in at least four situations. This is not politeness. These are cases where a boutique like 41 Labs is the wrong tool.

  • You need software, not only AI. A full mobile app, an eCommerce platform, a SaaS product, a corporate site rebuild. Techtiq builds all of that. 41 Labs does not.
  • You need many developers for a long stretch. Techtiq offers staff augmentation, dedicated teams, and IT staffing as a licensed employment agency, backed by 350+ engineers. 41 Labs cannot staff your team.
  • Your procurement checklist rewards years and certifications. Techtiq has operated since 2017, states ISO 27001 compliant processes, and names enterprise clients like Standard Chartered Bank and Abbott. A company founded in 2024 cannot match that paper trail, and for some buyers that matters.
  • Your project mixes AI with heavy non-AI work. Legacy modernization, cloud migration, and ongoing managed IT alongside an AI component. One vendor covering all of it can be simpler than two.

If two or more of those describe you, speak to Techtiq first. They are built for that shape of work.

When 41 Labs is the better choice

41 Labs is the stronger fit when your problem is one expensive process and you want it fixed fast, by a senior person, with proof before money changes hands.

  • You want the person who scoped it to build it. No account layers, no offshore handoffs, no juniors learning on your project. You deal with the founder from first call to go-live.
  • You want proof before payment. 41 Labs builds a free working demo on your real data first. You decide with a result in front of you, not a slide deck.
  • You want speed. Live in 4 to 8 weeks. The kind of outcome that matters here is concrete: quotes that took about 3 hours now take 4 minutes.
  • You want a known price and grant help. Projects from S$10,000, stated publicly. EDG covers up to 50% of qualifying costs, up to 70% for eligible SMEs, and 41 Labs handles the application paperwork.
  • Your problem is one process, not a program. Quoting, document processing, WhatsApp enquiries going cold. Narrow problems get better results from a narrow specialist.

Questions to ask either company before you sign

Whichever way you lean, ask both vendors the same six questions. The answers separate a good engagement from an expensive lesson.

  • Who exactly builds my system, and will I speak to them directly? At many firms the senior person sells and someone else builds. Get names.
  • Can I see a working result on my data before I commit money? A demo on your real quotes or documents tells you more than any case study.
  • What is the total price, and what would the EDG grant cover? If a vendor cannot talk grants, budget the full amount yourself. Our cost guide gives you reference numbers to check quotes against.
  • How long until it is live, and what counts as done? Ask for a date and a measurable outcome, like quote time or accuracy, not a phase plan.
  • Which of your live systems is closest to my use case, and can I speak to that client? Shipped and still running beats built and abandoned.
  • Where does my data go, and is the build PDPA compliant? Any serious Singapore vendor should answer this in one minute, including where models run and who can see your data.

Frequently asked questions

Is Techtiq Solutions a good AI company?

Based on public information, yes. Techtiq Solutions has operated since 2017, and its website reports 350+ engineers, 800+ delivered projects, clients such as Standard Chartered Bank and Abbott, and ISO 27001 compliant processes. Its AI practice covers chatbots, generative AI, computer vision, and automation. It is best suited to larger software builds and to companies that need extra developers on staff.

Who is the best AI development company in Singapore?

There is no single best AI company in Singapore, only the best fit for your situation. Full-service software houses like Techtiq Solutions fit larger builds that need many engineers across web, mobile, and AI. Boutique specialists like 41 Labs fit SMEs that want one process fixed fast, founder-led, live in 4 to 8 weeks from about S$10,000. Large consultancies fit enterprise-wide transformation.

How much does custom AI cost in Singapore?

Custom AI projects in Singapore start around S$10,000 with a boutique specialist like 41 Labs and run to six figures for enterprise programs. Techtiq Solutions does not publish pricing and quotes each project after a consultation. The EDG grant covers up to 50% of qualifying costs, and up to 70% for eligible SMEs, which often halves what you actually pay.

What is the difference between 41 Labs and Techtiq Solutions?

Scale and delivery model. Techtiq Solutions is a software house founded in 2017 with 350+ engineers, project management and QA in Singapore and development teams in Vietnam, covering web, mobile, eCommerce, IT staffing, and AI. 41 Labs is a boutique founded in 2024 that builds only AI systems, founder-led with no junior handoffs, with a free working demo before payment and go-live in 4 to 8 weeks.

Can I get a government grant for an AI project in Singapore?

Yes. The Enterprise Development Grant covers up to 50% of qualifying AI project costs, and up to 70% for eligible SMEs, administered by Enterprise Singapore. 41 Labs prepares the EDG paperwork for clients. Techtiq Solutions does not mention grant support on its website, so ask them directly if you shortlist them.
