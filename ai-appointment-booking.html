AI Appointment Booking — FAQ

What is AI appointment booking?

AI appointment booking uses an AI agent to schedule appointments automatically — answering enquiries, checking availability, booking, rescheduling, and sending reminders 24/7, without staff. A custom one, like 41 Labs builds, is trained on your services, durations, pricing, and availability rules, and integrates with your calendar and WhatsApp.

What is an AI receptionist?

An AI receptionist is an AI agent that handles the front-desk conversation — answering questions about services, prices, and availability, then booking, rescheduling, and reminding — across WhatsApp, web, and phone, 24/7. It captures the after-hours enquiries that would otherwise go to voicemail and never call back.

Which businesses is this for?

Clinics, dental and aesthetic practices, salons, physiotherapists, vets, tuition centres, and any service business that books appointments. We train the agent on your specific services, durations, and availability rules.

Does it integrate with my calendar and WhatsApp?

Yes. The AI receptionist integrates with your calendar and books on WhatsApp, web, and phone, confirming and reminding automatically so your schedule stays full and no-shows drop.

How much does an AI receptionist cost?

A custom AI booking agent from 41 Labs typically falls in the S$7,000–S$25,000 range depending on scope and integrations. You get a working prototype before paying, and eligible Singapore businesses can offset up to 50% via the EDG grant.
